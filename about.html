About AuraCraft

Crafting visual excellence since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, AuraCraft has grown from a small design studio to a full-service creative agency.

We believe in the power of visual storytelling to transform brands and connect with audiences. Our team of passionate designers, developers, and strategists work together to create memorable experiences that drive results.

With offices in 3 countries and clients across the globe, we've helped over 500 brands elevate their visual identity and digital presence.

Our Journey

2010 - Foundation

Founded as a small design studio with just 3 team members working from a home office.

2012 - First Major Client

Landed our first Fortune 500 client, marking our transition to professional studio.

2015 - Expansion

Opened our second office in London and expanded our team to 15 creative professionals.

2018 - Digital Transformation

Launched our digital services division to meet growing client demand for web and app design.

2022 - Rebrand

Rebranded as AuraCraft to better reflect our collaborative approach and family values.
